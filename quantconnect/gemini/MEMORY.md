# Gemini Strategy Scouting Memory

This folder contains the results and artifacts of the 50-iteration strategy scouting loop performed in May 2026.


## Files
- `visited.txt`: Log of all researched URLs to avoid duplication.
- `strategies_found.md`: Summary of strategies identified in the community.
- qc_forum_1.md: Results for iterations 1-50.
- qc_forum_2.md: Results for iterations 51-100.
- algos/it_*.py: Individual strategy implementations from the 100 iterations.

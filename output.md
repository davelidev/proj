# Session Insights & Reports - April 25, 2026

## Strategy Analysis: What Works vs. What Doesn't

### The Winners (30%+ CAGR)
*   **Regime Switching (SMA 200):** The most effective filter. Treating Bull and Bear markets as different environments is the primary alpha source for TQQQ/SOXL.
*   **Short-Term RSI Dips:** Buying extreme oversold conditions (RSI2 < 25) during a structural bull market consistently outperforms breakout strategies.
*   **Dynamic Leverage:** Reducing exposure at overbought peaks (RSI10 > 80) preserves capital better than binary all-in/all-out signals.

### The Losers
*   **Pure Breakouts:** TQQQ's volatility leads to frequent whipsaws; buying new highs often results in entering at the peak of a short-term move.
*   **Over-Filtering:** Stacking too many "AND" conditions reduces trade frequency to the point where the strategy misses the essential growth years.
*   **Lagging Indicators:** Slow averages (TriAverage) and ADX-heavy entries often enter too late, allowing volatility decay to erode profits before the trend is captured.

### Key Strategic Recommendation
Shift towards **"Selling into Strength"** (Price Targets/High RSI) rather than "Trailing Stops," as TQQQ's natural volatility frequently triggers stops prematurely.

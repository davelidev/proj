**Description:** Uses the 25-period Aroon oscillator (AroonUp minus AroonDown) on QQQ as a trend direction filter. Holds leveraged Nasdaq when the oscillator is positive—meaning a recent high was set more recently than a recent low—and parks in T-bills when trend reverses.

*Overfit 2/10 — Single textbook indicator at a standard period (25) with a zero-line threshold — minimal tuning*

- **Entry:** Aroon(25) oscillator > 0 (AroonUp − AroonDown > 0): 100% TQQQ
- **Exit:** Aroon(25) oscillator ≤ 0: 100% BIL
- **Symbols:** Signal & Execution: QQQ / TQQQ / BIL

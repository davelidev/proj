## Strategy-1
### Aroon(25) Oscillator>0 TQQQ (001.py)

**Description:** Holds leveraged Nasdaq when QQQ's Aroon oscillator (25-period AroonUp minus AroonDown) is positive, indicating that the most recent 25-day high is more recent than the most recent 25-day low. Exits to T-bills when the oscillator turns negative.

*Overfit 2/10 — Single Aroon(25) oscillator at zero-cross — textbook period and canonical threshold.*

- **Entry:** Aroon(25) oscillator > 0 (AroonUp > AroonDown): 100% TQQQ
- **Exit:** Aroon(25) oscillator ≤ 0: 100% BIL
- **Symbols:** Signal: QQQ. Execution: TQQQ / BIL
- **Rebalance:** Daily, 30 min after market open (only on regime change)

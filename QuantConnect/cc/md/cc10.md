### Mom40+CCI+Top3 (cc3_354.py)

**Description:** A hybrid momentum rotator that replaces the oscillator component with CCI, using its zero-line crossover as the bullish signal. A positive CCI reading indicates the asset is trading above its average price level. The strategy otherwise follows the same three-signal scoring system and allocation ladder between leveraged tech, large-cap equities, and cash.

*Overfit 4/10 — Three components: 200-bar median gate, 40-day momentum, CCI(20) at zero threshold. CCI zero-line is a natural midpoint but period of 20 is slightly non-standard (14 is more canonical).*

- **Trend gate:** QQQ price > rolling 200-day median
- **Strength filter:** QQQ 40-day momentum positive AND CCI(20) > 0
- **Entry:** Score 3 → 100% TQQQ; Score 2 → 50% TQQQ + 50% top-3; Score 1 → 100% top-3; Score 0 → 50% top-3 + 50% BIL
- **Exit:** Signal score change triggers rebalance
- **Symbols:** Signal: QQQ + CCI(20). Execution: TQQQ / top-3 market-cap stocks / BIL
- **Universe:** Top 3 stocks by market cap from top 100 by dollar volume
- **Rebalance:** Daily, 30 min after market open, on state change only

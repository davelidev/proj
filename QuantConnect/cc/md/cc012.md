## Strategy-1
### Mom20+WILR14+Top5 w60 (001.py)

**Description:** A hybrid trend-momentum rotator that scores three signals on QQQ — a 200-day price-median trend gate, a short-term price momentum check, and a Williams %R reading — to assign one of three risk levels. When all signals align, it holds leveraged tech; partial agreement shifts into the top large-cap stocks; full disagreement mixes stocks and cash.

*Overfit 4/10 — Uses two standard components (price-median trend gate and WILR) plus a rate-of-change momentum check. The WILR threshold at -50 (midline) and the 200-day median-price regime filter are slightly unconventional. The mid-state blends (60/40 TQQQ/TopN) and BIL half-allocation at n=0 are tuned parameters.*

- **Trend gate:** QQQ price > median of last 200 daily closes
- **Strength filter:** Williams %R(14) on QQQ > -50
- **Entry:** Score 3 (all signals bullish) → 100% TQQQ; Score 2 → 60% TQQQ + 40% Top-5 large-caps equally; Score 1 → 100% Top-5 large-caps; Score 0 → 50% Top-5 + 50% BIL
- **Symbols:** Signal: QQQ + Williams %R. Execution: TQQQ / top-5 market-cap stocks from top-100 by dollar volume / BIL
- **Universe:** Coarse: top 100 by dollar volume; Fine: top 5 by market cap
- **Rebalance:** Daily, 30 min after market open, on state change only

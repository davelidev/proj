### Mom20+60+Med+Top1 (001.py)

**Description:** A 4-state hybrid rotator that counts three independent bullish conditions on QQQ — a 200-bar price median trend gate, a 20-day return, and a 60-day return — and maps the count to allocations across TQQQ, the largest-cap stock, and cash. Full agreement produces 100% TQQQ; partial agreement blends in the top market-cap stock; no agreement moves to cash via BIL. The universe selection targets the top dollar-volume stocks, narrowed to a single large-cap holding.

*Overfit 4/10 — Three momentum inputs (median gate, 20-day, 60-day) with non-standard allocation weights at n=3 (1.0/0.0/0.0), n=2 (0.7/0.3/0.0), n=1 (0.0/0.5/0.5), and n=0 (0.0/0.0/1.0). The 0.7/0.3 split at n=2 is custom-tuned.*

- **Trend gate:** QQQ price > 200-bar historical median
- **Entry:** n = count of: [trend gate, 20-day positive return, 60-day positive return]; n=3 → 100% TQQQ; n=2 → 70% TQQQ+30% Top-1; n=1 → 50% Top-1+50% BIL; n=0 → 100% BIL
- **Symbols:** Signal: QQQ. Execution: TQQQ / largest market-cap stock / BIL
- **Rebalance:** Daily, 30 min after market open; trades only on state change

**Description:** A 4-state momentum-volume hybrid that pairs a 200-bar price median trend gate and a 20-day return with a custom OBV slope signal on QQQ.
